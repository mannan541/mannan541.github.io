Policy for Blood Bank App

Effective Date: 01-06-2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Blood Bank App collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information of users who use the app. Protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring that your personal information remains secure and used only for official purposes. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal information.

Information We Collect:

1.1 Personal Information:

We collect the following personal information from you when you use our app:

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Age
  • Blood group
  • Address
  • Email address

1.2 Automatically Collected Information:

We may also collect certain information automatically when you use the app, such as your device's unique identifier, IP address, operating system information, and app usage data. This information is used for statistical analysis and to improve the functionality and performance of the app.

Use of Personal Information:

2.1 Official Purposes:

We use the personal information you provide solely for official purposes related to the functioning of the blood bank, including, but not limited to:

Displaying the details of blood donors, including name, contact number, age, blood group, address, and email address within the app for registered users of the blood bank to contact potential donors. Contacting you directly in case of emergency situations or to inform you about important updates related to the blood bank.

2.2 Statistical Analysis:

We may aggregate and anonymize your personal information to perform statistical analysis and gain insights into blood donor demographics, which helps us improve our services and better meet the needs of blood recipients.

Data Security:

3.1 Protection Measures:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include physical, electronic, and managerial safeguards designed to maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal information.

3.2 Data Retention:

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Sharing of Personal Information:

4.1 Limited Disclosure:

We will not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances: We may share your information with authorized third-party service providers who assist us in operating the blood bank app and providing related services. These service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are only permitted to use your information for the purposes specified by us. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights, property, or safety. Your Choices and Rights:

5.1 Access and Correction:

You have the right to access, review, and correct your personal information stored by the app. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us using the contact information provided in Section 7.

5.2 Withdrawal of Consent:

You may withdraw your consent for us to use your personal information at any time by contacting us using the contact information provided in Section 7. Please note that the withdrawal of consent may limit or prevent us from providing certain services to you.

5.2 Withdrawal of Consent:

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be posted on this page, and the revised version will become effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
